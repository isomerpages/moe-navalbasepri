<p>DigitalAccess@Home provides subsidised broadband and laptops/tablets to
lower-income households to support their lifestyle needs, including work
and social activities.</p>
<p>For more information about the DigitalAccess@Home, please visit <a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home</a>

<p><strong>Who can apply?</strong>
<br>Low-income households living in HDB flats with at least one Singapore
Citizen are eligible to apply for DigitalAccess@Home. You can check your
eligibility at this website <a href="https://go.gov.sg/digitalaccesschecker" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/digitalaccesschecker</a>.</p>
<p>Click on <a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home/scheme-details#ef97571b-3546-4a23-83a1-2815b62d1cfc" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Scheme Details</a> for
more information.

<p><strong>How can I apply?</strong>
<br>You can apply in two ways:
<br>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Log in to digitalaccess.gov.sg via <a href="https://eservice.imda.gov.sg/das/singpasslogin?strParam=login&amp;notprotected=true" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Singpass.</a>
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hard copy submission (by mail): Download and print the <a href="https://file.go.gov.sg/digitalaccess-application-form.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">DigitalAccess@Home form here</a>,
complete it and mail it to IMDA.
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>For further enquiries, you may contact IMDA via <a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/about-imda/contact-us" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.imda.gov.sg/about-imda/contact-us</a>
</p>
